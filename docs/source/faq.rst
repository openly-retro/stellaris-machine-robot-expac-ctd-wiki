Frequently Asked Questions
##########################

What's the difference between Machines and Robots?
==================================================

_And Mechanicals for that matter?_

**TL;DR: Robots are non-sentient assembled species, Mechanicals are generally Synths (formerly bio, and now are manufactured), and Machines are sentient, assembled, and can take any form**

In Stellaris, the line is slightly blurred between Machines, Robots, and "Mechanicals". 

- Robots seem to generally be created by some other species, and aren't sentient.
- Machines are similar in that generally they were created "at some point" and have sentience.
- Mechanicals are analogous to "Synthetic" life forms, that used to be biological and now they live in manufactured versions of their original bodies (after ascension).

In much of the game, the "Mechanical" text has been replaced with "Robot". There are also technologies that upgrade Robots to 'Droids'.. it can be confusing.

These are my personal definitions as a sci-fi writer, which I apply to this mod, having also delved into Stellaris' lore regarding the topic. The :ref:`ap_bio_mech` species is a custom one from this mod:
